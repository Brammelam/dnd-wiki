# Session 14 — Salt, Thorn & Cheese

## Recap

[Voris](/characters/voris) and [Peren](/characters/peren) traveled from [Dior](/places/dior) toward [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep) while [Fenris](/characters/fenris) and [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) dealt with the [Burning of Sire](/events/burning-of-sire).

### The Road

They stayed at an inn called **The Thirsty Dwarf** outside the city, then continued along the river through flat, dusty terrain. Voris was weighed down by weapons while Peren moved lighter. They passed farms, fisher folk, and growing crowds of travelers all heading toward Morgan's Keep.

At a roadside inn, all rooms were full — they slept in the stable. The meal was memorable: sausages, root vegetables, good bread, and a **distinctive local cheese** that became a running theme.

### Meeting Lynn Salt

A familiar face entered the inn: **[Lynn Salt](/characters/lynn-salt)**, a dwarven woman with curly hair, a backpack, and a book. They had met her before. She was also heading to Morgan's Keep, transporting magical ingredients for [the Academy](/factions/the-academy). She was businesslike and reserved, not offering much detail about her work.

She gave a lesson on local **cheese-making** — how it was made from a cow named Klara.

### Meeting Thor

An old man named **Thor** sat at their table with his own cheese. Peren asked for a piece. Thor was heading to Morgan's Keep for work and shared stories: drought had hit the brewing industry, and there were rumors of **ghosts and strange happenings** around Fortrejon, with a name "Kopp" whispered in connection.

### Card Stories

The party drew storytelling cards. [Peren](/characters/peren) told a formative memory: as a child in the forest, he witnessed mercenaries commit terrible violence — a sight that shattered him but also lit a desire to protect others. He admitted to being a criminal who tries to do good. An old voice at the table spoke about *"an eye for an eye"* and the tension between justice and forgiveness.

### The Potion Shop

Voris and Peren visited a traveling herbalist. They:
- Discussed magical glasses — Voris always wanted a far-seeing pair
- Examined **fey pollen** and **dried surbla** leaf
- The herbalist explained that pollen potions have unpredictable effects: healing, but with possible side effects like skin discoloration or strange smells
- Peren **impulsively threw a reagent into the fire**, creating a blinding flash that temporarily blinded him and ruined the surbla potion
- They commissioned a **fairy healing potion** from the surviving ingredients
- Peren discovered he has a basic talent for potion-making

### Ambush in the Night

The herbalist (actually the innkeeper's old associate) **poisoned their drinks**, causing deep sleep. Around four hours later, Peren woke groggy and was **stabbed in the back** by an attacker in the stable.

A brutal night fight erupted:
- Peren wrestled free and pinned his attacker with a knife to the throat
- [Voris](/characters/voris) woke, drew his rapier, and **killed the first attacker** with rapid, merciless thrusts
- A second attacker threw a **flashbang**, panicking the horses
- Voris fired two arrows, hitting the attacker in the back and shoulder
- Peren was **knocked unconscious** by the combined effects of poison and wounds
- Voris administered first aid and a healing potion

The defeated attacker confessed: he had poisoned them with a sleep compound. He was bitter rather than evil, nearly senile but dangerous. Peren took his **poisoning notebook** and some daggers.

### Departure

A worried woman (the innkeeper's wife) arrived. The party showed their credentials and were asked to leave. They joined **[Lynn Salt](/characters/lynn-salt)**'s wagon for the remaining journey to Morgan's Keep, acquiring two horses from the stable in the process.

The session ended with the party traveling the final stretch toward the capital.
