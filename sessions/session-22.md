## Recap

[Fenris](/characters/fenris) and [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) rode toward the smoke while [Voris](/characters/voris) and [Peren](/characters/peren) headed to [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep).

### The Journey

They rode along the river through evening and night, passing small farms. The smell of burning grew stronger. After hours, they camped at a clearing by the riverbank. The next morning, riders in fine but sweaty clothes passed — tired and worried refugees. They learned the forest across the river was burning and houses near the treeline had been consumed. No word had come from [Kalén](/places/kalen).

As the day wore on, more refugees streamed past: mothers with children, elderly on donkeys, men carrying what little they could save. A panicked man galloped toward them with wide eyes.

### The Rescue

A small girl came running, covered in ash, screaming that her father was trapped and would burn. They rode through pockets of fire — Fenris calmed the reluctant horse — and found **[Knut Pilsen](/characters/knut-pilsen)** pinned under a fallen burning tree near the river, his hair on fire.

- [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) threw her cloak over the flames and stamped them out, **fighting through her personal fear of fire**
- [Fenris](/characters/fenris) carried a floating mass of water from the river and doused the flames
- Fenris transformed into a **bear** and lifted the tree
- They carried Knut to the river and healed him with magic
- Knut called for his horse **Betty**, who had fled across the river

### Sire

Approaching the town, Fenris followed Betty's hoof prints and found the horses among burning textiles, calming them and sending them across the river.

[Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) plunged into the burning town looking for salvage. In a smith's workshop, she found **two rapiers** under debris. She grabbed them as the building collapsed behind her in a shower of sparks and dust.

### The Pyromancer Fight

Emerging from the smithy, Gal'Rok was hit by a **fireball from the town square**. A scarred, burned man stood there holding a book, smoke swirling around him in unnatural patterns.

**Round 1:** Gal'Rok charged and **severed his hand** — the one holding the book. He retaliated with a point-blank fire blast, sending her flying.

**Round 2:** Fenris crossed a burning bridge as it collapsed cinematically behind him. He transformed into a **giant constricting snake** and slithered toward the pyromancer. Gal'Rok recovered and struck again with a devastating axe blow, the glass axe shattering into magical shards.

**Round 3:** The pyromancer drank from a bottle, raised an invisible barrier against Fenris's snake form, and unleashed a final **explosion of fire point-blank**. Both Fenris and Gal'Rok were knocked unconscious and thrown through the air. The pyromancer's legs were burned to stumps — he died in his own blast.

### Aftermath

Fenris crawled to consciousness, found a **glowing medallion** among the remains (too hot to hold, wrapped in cloth), and collected burned book pages. Gal'Rok woke in a **PTSD panic**, running from the flames before Fenris healed her. They found a dead body with a purse marked **"Sigurd"**.

With the medallion and evidence secured, they headed back toward the horses.
