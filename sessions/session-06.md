## Recap

In [Rinden](/places/rinden)'s **Old Town**, Varis, Flint, Fenris, and Elowen sought passage to **[Scully Stronghold](/places/scully-stronghold)** — a fortress-island on the horizon where nobility gathered behind [Lady Amber](/characters/lady-amber)'s invitations. A flat barge ("lektør") crossed the water, powered by donkeys walking in circles; only invited guests could board.

The party scouted the chaotic docks. **Fenris** considered Wild Shape as a rat stowed in cargo; **Elowen** could turn someone **invisible** for an hour. They spotted a **familiar mage** in the noble queue. Elowen approached [Brad](/characters/brad-academy-student), spun a tale of searching for a lost love on the stronghold, and secured passage as his guest.

The layered plan took shape: Fenris as a **rat in a crate**; Elowen escorted by the mage; **Varis made invisible** by Elowen's spell to board unseen; Flint to blend with workers or find another way aboard. Rain fell as the barge's next departure approached — a tense, multi-threaded infiltration of a closed noble redoubt.
