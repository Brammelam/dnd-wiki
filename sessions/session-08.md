## Recap

After stabilizing **Flint** and **Peren**, **Varis** and **Elowen** prepared to leave for **Dior** and answers about their imprisonment. Varis haggled for a **green cloak** and, with **Detect Magic**, confirmed a merchant's **thunder-blessed arrow** was real — while exposing a fake "vampiric" dagger, angering the shopkeeper.

At the city gates, guard **Markus** recognized Varis from military days and delayed them with nostalgia. Markus's unit was bound for **Fort John** amid local unrest; he invited Varis to visit. Outside the walls, starving refugees were turned away while priests distributed food — the pair **donated gold**.

**Brad** (Academy student) asked them to deliver a **locked metal box** to **Professor Toltec** in Dior for **40 gold**. They accepted the courier job and continued north, weighing the box's unknown risk.

## Key Events
