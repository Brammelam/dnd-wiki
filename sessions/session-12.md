## Recap

**Elowen** touched a **crystal** and met **Mirbog**, a half-orc guardian sent by **Titania** to supervise **yet another braid** along the planar fault the party chipped at during the **[Lake Hydra Hunt](/events/lake-hydra-hunt)**—*[Fenris](/characters/fenris) kept **Blade of the Ethereal** and Elowen already bore **Sprout** + **Nathair's Mask** from that delve; Mirbog's focus fell on **Nathair's Mask** channeling this gate instead of gifting it anew.* A ritual unfolded; the lattice became **spear-like**, dangerously **healing** nearby flesh—even **reviving** a fallen half-orc at a dreadful price.

The party realized removing planar relics could still unleash **dead tides** earlier than comfortable; Mirbog gave a **formula to reseal** the threatened breach and departed. They pressed toward **Fort John**, now a **rebel-occupied** siege camp: **Karl Marsensen**'s arrest had sparked revolt. **Lieutenant Markus** and **Baron Peridott** hired them as "independent agents" — **5 gp** each to extract Karl, **15 gp** to solve the crisis, plus a **masterwork bow** rumor.

Plans ranged from **diplomacy** with Karl's wife **Frida**, **Fenris** as giant spider, **illusions**, to **sabotage** of supplies. The fortress's rusted hinges, side doors, and river approaches were scouted.
