## Recap

The party exited a **[Fey Realm](/places/fey-realm) portal**. [Flint](/characters/flint) had already been badly injured in the Fey Realm and was slowing the group. In the campaign's most traumatic beat, Flint reached back through the closing portal for his hammer — and the **portal severed his arm at the elbow**. He stayed behind to recover while the others pressed on.

At **[Hooves Bay](/places/hooves-bay)**, a boy begged help with **pirates**; city guards did nothing, and the party moved on.

That night, **[Sommerhimmel](/characters/sommerhimmel)** punished [Elowen](/characters/elowen) for deceiving the fey, sending her after **hidden treasure** in a grove. The trail revealed a **secret phrase**, an **ancient elven military outpost**, armory **loot**, a **ghost**, a **sealed portal to [Dead Isle](/places/dead-isle)**, and **crystals** by an underground lake. When Elowen claimed the **largest crystal**, she saw a vision of a **masked woman** who once guarded the site.
