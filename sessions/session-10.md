## Recap

With two days until the wedding, the party shifted from brute-force plans to reconnaissance and infiltration of the temple on [Dior](/places/dior)'s lake.

### Temple Infiltration

Workers were setting up chairs and decorations at the temple. Guards stood at the entrance with a large dog.

- **[Peren](/characters/peren)** tried to bluff his way in as a worker — the guard wasn't fooled
- **[Fenris](/characters/fenris)** transformed into a **cat** and snuck in through the crowd
- The cat explored the altar and columns, but accidentally knocked over a large flower vase — creating a loud crash that drew everyone's attention
- Using the chaos, Peren slipped past the guards and explored the **basement**: three doors on each side, a locked room, storage, and a dining area with workers. Five doors total, the nearest locked. The rooms extended under the building and partially under the waterline
- [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) used the confusion to climb up and survey from above
- [Voris](/characters/voris) stood watch outside

The cat (Fenris) continued causing havoc — climbing curtains, scratching a servant who swung a broom at him, and eventually escaping through the crowd.

### Following Helga

[Helga](/characters/helga) appeared outside the temple, surrounded by soldiers, giving orders. The party recognized her and began surveillance.

[Fenris](/characters/fenris) (as a cat) **followed Helga** through the city for hours, learning her routines. She moved between the temple, a workshop, and a grand manor house guarded by well-armed, ceremonial soldiers. The manor had walls, guard posts, and limited entry points.

### Equipment Shopping

- **[Bob Swords](/characters/bob-swords)** promised to get blueprints of the temple: wall thickness, drainage, interior rooms
- They purchased **elemental arrows** (fire, ice, lightning), a **grappling hook**, and supplies
- At **Nymph's Tears** shop (run by a bald hobbit), they bought healing potions, a strong laxative potion, and a milder one — at negotiated prices
- They discussed using sleep and laxative potions to incapacitate Helga or wedding guests

### Peren's Investigation

[Peren](/characters/peren) (as a cat) tried to infiltrate Helga's manor by catching mice near the guards, hoping to be adopted as a pet. The guards were friendly but he couldn't find an entry point. The manor was too well-built and guarded.

### The Knives

Peren examined the **cursed rust knives** from the crypt. Each cut opens a wound that bleeds worse with each activation — stacking damage that becomes severe with multiple cuts. Peren admitted his blood aversion makes these weapons especially problematic for him personally.

The session ended with one day remaining before the wedding, plans still forming.
