## Recap

The full party arrived in **[Dior](/places/dior)** after four or five days: mountain tunnel, statues, circular valley, artificial lake, temple on the water, **morning** and **evening** districts. They took rooms at **[Mountain Dew's Rest](/places/mountain-dews-rest)** and learned of strict height laws and the **Academy** on the morning side.

At **[the Academy](/places/the-academy)**, **Gilda** haggled hard over Fenris's **glasses**, finally paying **300 gold**. They scheduled **[Toltec](/characters/toltec)** for **7 PM** to deliver the **mysterious box**. At **[Bob Swords](/characters/bob-swords)** they priced armor; **[Tore Idle](/characters/tore-idle)** repaired Varis's bow for **50 gold**. Bob pointed them to **[Carlos](/characters/carlos)** on the shadow side.

Carlos offered **25% + fence fee** for loot from the **700-year sealed crypt**; his bad knee kept him out. At night he led them through a **cellar** near the wedding-prep temple, past loose planks into the rock. Inside: dwarven runes, **four statue door puzzle**, and a **light-trap** that dislocated **Gal'Rok**'s shoulder before they cut her free. The delve had begun.
