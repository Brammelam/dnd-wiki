## Recap

The session opened with the magical trap's vine-like tendrils seizing [Fenris](/characters/fenris)'s hands as the door slammed shut, separating [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) on the other side. [Voris](/characters/voris) and [Peren](/characters/peren) frantically pulled Fenris free and rolled clear.

### Exploring the Burial Chambers

The corridor was lined with stone coffins bearing dwarven inscriptions. Voris lit a torch, revealing dusty, sealed caskets. Exploring the fifteen coffins on each side yielded **19 ancient coins** with intricate engravings and **12 short, rusty hammers**. Fenris shifted into bear form to help move the heavy lids.

### The River Crossing

The passage ended at a **10-meter gap** over a steaming underground river with jagged rocks below. Using Fenris's climbing rope, Peren's archery, and an entangle spell, they improvised a rope bridge. [Peren](/characters/peren) crossed first with rogue-like grace. [Voris](/characters/voris) followed with a theatrical backflip. [Fenris](/characters/fenris) nearly fell when the damp rope slipped, but Peren rescued him with an acrobatic sprint along the rope.

### The Underground City

Beyond the river, the tunnels opened into an **enormous cylindrical cavern** — a dwarven city built into the walls, with houses on both sides of a broad main street, rising in tiers. The air was heavy and claustrophobic.

They split up to search the buildings. [Peren](/characters/peren) was ambushed by **three small green goblins**. A quick, brutal fight followed — Peren stabbed one down, Voris used Second Wind and charged in. One goblin teleported with an ethereal flash before reappearing elsewhere. They killed the goblins and found signs of a small goblin settlement: mushrooms, coins, small sleeping areas.

### The Fire Elemental

On a central platform, they found a raised sarcophagus guarded by four dwarf statues with weapons and strange symbols. A mushroom patch grew around it. When [Peren](/characters/peren) took the **knives** from a statue, a fire elemental erupted.

The battle was devastating:
- [Fenris](/characters/fenris) raised a **wind wall** that damaged both the elemental and Peren
- Peren caught fire and took ongoing burn damage, fighting with only 6 HP
- He activated **Fighting Spirit** for temporary HP and attack advantage
- Fenris healed Peren with a quick spell — *"You're dying, bro!"*
- Voris **spat water** at the elemental as a taunt
- The elemental phased through the party, slashing with fire
- Peren used Sneak Attack with the cursed knives, but the elemental was **resistant to physical damage**
- Fenris shifted into a **massive polar bear**, biting and clawing while taking fire damage
- Voris played a bone flute — the *Song of the Forest* — summoning **spectral water spirits** that drove the elemental into the stone

### The Dragon

After the elemental fled, a **mushroom patch** grew around the sarcophagus. Then the **ceiling exploded** — a massive reddish dragon claw emerged. A dragon head appeared, flames licking. Fenris approached in awe: *"Magnificent."* The dragon responded with a **fireball** directly at him, knocking him unconscious.

The session ended with Voris and Peren standing in dust and smoke, needing to decide their next move with the dragon looming and Fenris down.
