## Recap

**[Salander](/characters/salander)** received the party after their work and shared intelligence: the Crown Prince arrest looked like a **setup**; **[Helga](/characters/helga)** of the King's Guard was implicated, and she might be found in **[Dior](/places/dior)** for an upcoming wedding. He pointed them toward **[Bob Swords](/characters/bob-swords)** as a possible ally. The name "Countess Barros" proved to be a **fabrication**.

Salander asked them to **assassinate** a guard blocking his interests; led by Varis's ethics, they **refused**. **Griselda**, a tough dwarf with a broken arm, offered another job: **intimidate delinquent tenants** in Mud Street — with pay and introductions to poison contacts for Peren.

On **Mud Street**, Varis kicked in a door where **hobbits** shielded tenants from rent. A brutal brawl followed: holy water and clubs, **Peren's poison bluff**, Varis's **Sharpshooter** volleys, Flint's **Vow of Enmity** and warhammer. The hobbits and locals were beaten into submission; Griselda reminded them the goal was leverage, not ruining her income.

## Key Events

- Intelligence on Helga, Dior, and Bob Swords
- Refused assassination contract
- Debt-collection fight in Mud Street
