**Thorn's serpent hunt** was [Griselda](/characters/griselda-mud-street)'s thread to a reclusive poison expert, **[Thorn](/characters/thorn-alchemist)**, who lived by the sea. He taught [Elowen](/characters/elowen) recipes (including **Dobbelsvin**) and sent the party to an **island sea serpent** for venom.

To isolate **[Peren](/characters/peren)** against the beast, Thorn **treated the oars** with a **sleeping potion** so multiple party members **fainted** when they landed on the island. [Elowen](/characters/elowen) **destroyed his front door** in a rage over the betrayal. The serpent fight was still brutal — poison clouds and bites — but they returned with venom for further training.

## Visions

[Elowen](/characters/elowen) suffered visions: caged beings **Grimm** and **Bobble** in flame, tied to the [Fey Realm](/places/fey-realm). A future ritual might need a friend's stick, three gold coins, and a life — interpreted with grim care.
