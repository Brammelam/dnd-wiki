**The Crabs Job** was [Peren](/characters/peren) and [Voris](/characters/voris)'s infiltration of the criminal underworld in [Rinden](/places/rinden) to find information about their framing.

## The Investigation

After selling the Azure Oak seed, Peren and Voris began investigating who framed them for the [Crown Prince's murder](/events/crown-prince-murder):

1. **[Toll Guild](/factions/toll-guild) HQ** — The clerk was unhelpful and bureaucratic
2. **[The Crabs](/factions/the-crabs)** — A criminal gang that could provide underground intelligence

## Earning Trust

To gain access to [The Crabs](/factions/the-crabs), Peren and Voris:
- **Watched fight pits** — Underground combat rings run by the gang
- **Saved a Crabs member** — Intervened when one of their own was in danger
- [Peren](/characters/peren) received a **poison-making course** — Practical skills from the gang

## The Deal

Their work earned a meeting with [Salander](/characters/salander), the gang's leader. He offered:
- He would **investigate who framed the party** using his information network
- In return, the party had to **expose the shady business** of [Father Terry](/characters/father-terry)

The outcome of this deal remains pending as the party left [Rinden](/places/rinden) before completing the task.
