The **Fort John rebellion** was a regional crisis sparked by **[Karl Marsensen](/characters/karl-marsensen)**'s arrest. Rebels held the fort; **[Baron Peridott](/characters/baron-peridott)** and **[Markus](/characters/markus-lieutenant)** hired the party as independent agents to extract Karl or resolve the standoff.

## Portal and mask

[Elowen](/characters/elowen) touched a **crystal** and met **[Mirbog](/characters/mirbog)**, a half-orc guardian serving **Titania**, who kept a **portal** sealed with three relics. Elowen took the **mask** (boosting illusion magic). Removing relics risked a **dead horde**; Mirbog left a **formula to reseal** the gate.

## Inside the fort

[Fenris](/characters/fenris) infiltrated as a squirrel, was caged, then escaped after casting *entangle*. [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) navigated the **beer cellar** with rebels **Berg**, **Frank**, and **Sofia**, and **[Frida Marsensen](/characters/frida-marsensen)**'s leadership. [Voris](/characters/voris) **sabotaged beer casks**; Fenris **tainted the well**. Negotiations tipped toward surrender with **limited rations**.

## Aftermath

**[Konstantin Carnelian](/characters/konstantin-carnelian)**, a bounty hunter for **[Lady Alabaster](/characters/lady-alabaster)**, hunted the party over a **Tom** murder accusation; Markus helped them evade him. The baron paid **13 gp** each. [Voris](/characters/voris) chose a **non-magical masterwork bow**. The party rode toward [Dior](/places/dior) with **Brad's metal box**, hearing that **Helga** waited ahead.
