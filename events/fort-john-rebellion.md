The **Fort John rebellion** was a regional crisis sparked by **[Karl Marsensen](/characters/karl-marsensen)**'s arrest. Rebels held the fort; **[Baron Peridott](/characters/baron-peridott)** and **[Markus](/characters/markus-lieutenant)** hired the party as independent agents to extract Karl or resolve the standoff.

## Portal echo (Mirbog)

[Elowen](/characters/elowen) contacted **[Mirbog](/characters/mirbog)** via **another fey-linked crystal**, rehearsing fallout from planar relics ripped from the **[Lake Hydra Hunt](/events/lake-hydra-hunt)** ward—**[Fenris](/characters/fenris)** bore **Blade of the Ethereal** and Elowen **Sprout** + **Nathair's Mask** before this audience. Ritual repercussions still threatened undeath tides; Mirbog inscribed a **resealing formula** for worst-case ruptures ([Session 12](/sessions/session-12)).

## Inside the fort

[Fenris](/characters/fenris) infiltrated as a squirrel, was caged, then escaped after casting *entangle*. [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) navigated the **beer cellar** with rebels **Berg**, **Frank**, and **Sofia**, and **[Frida Marsensen](/characters/frida-marsensen)**'s leadership. [Varis](/characters/varis) **sabotaged beer casks**; Fenris **tainted the well**. Negotiations tipped toward surrender with **limited rations**.

## Aftermath

**[Konstantin Carnelian](/characters/konstantin-carnelian)**, a bounty hunter for **[Lady Alabaster](/characters/lady-alabaster)**, hunted the party over a **Tom** murder accusation; Markus helped them evade him. The baron paid **13 gp** each. [Varis](/characters/varis) chose a **non-magical masterwork bow**. The party rode toward [Dior](/places/dior) with **Brad's metal box**, hearing that **Helga** waited ahead.
