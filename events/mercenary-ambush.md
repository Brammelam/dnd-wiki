The **Mercenary Ambush** occurred when [Fat Hans](/characters/fat-hans) and his hired fighters attacked the party on the road.

## Background

During the [escape from Morgan's Keep](/events/escape-from-morgans-keep), the party killed wall guards who turned out to be **sons of a countess**. Enraged, the countess hired [Fat Hans](/characters/fat-hans), a former guard turned mercenary, and his squad to hunt them down.

## The Ambush

After a cart crash on the road, Fat Hans and his mercenaries caught up to the party. Hans revealed the reason for the pursuit: the countess wanted revenge for her dead sons.

## Outcome

The party won the fight decisively:
- Defeated all the mercenaries
- Stole a **horse**
- Took a **compass** and other gear
- Learned the full story of why they were being hunted
- Tied Fat Hans and surviving mercenaries **to a tree**

## Significance

This encounter revealed an additional layer of consequences from the escape — not only were they wanted by the crown, but a wealthy noble had placed a private bounty on them. The stolen compass would later play a role in tracking [Helga](/characters/helga) during the [Dior wedding plot](/events/dior-wedding).
