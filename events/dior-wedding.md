The **Dior Wedding** plot spanned three sessions of elaborate espionage, culminating in a dramatic confrontation with [Helga](/characters/helga) at the [Tyr Moir](/places/tyr-moir) graveyard.

## The Wedding

A grand wedding was being prepared at the temple in the center of [Dior](/places/dior)'s lake. [Helga](/characters/helga), the [King's Guard](/factions/kings-guard) captain who had arrested the party, was present overseeing security and logistics.

## Reconnaissance (Session 10)

The party spent days surveilling and infiltrating:

- **Temple infiltration** — [Fenris](/characters/fenris) entered as a cat, causing chaos by knocking over flower vases to create diversions. [Peren](/characters/peren) used the distraction to explore the basement, discovering training rooms, locked doors, and passages partially underwater.
- **Following Helga** — [Fenris](/characters/fenris) shadowed Helga as a cat through the city, discovering her grand house guarded by well-armed soldiers.
- **Equipment gathering** — They purchased elemental arrows, potions (including laxatives), disguise materials, and a grappling hook from [Bob Swords](/characters/bob-swords) and the Nymph's Tears shop.
- **Wedding invitations** — Obtained through [Bob Swords](/characters/bob-swords).

## The Plan (Session 11)

The party decided against direct violence at the wedding and instead planned to **lure Helga** to a private meeting:

- Rented formal clothes from Griselda's "The Blue Cloak"
- Purchased cosmetics for disguise
- [Peren](/characters/peren) created **illusory rats** with a flute to distract guards at the temple
- [Fenris](/characters/fenris) (as a cat) **planted a compass** in Helga's belt pouch to track her movements
- Sent an anonymous threat via magical ring: *"Meet at Tyr Moir, at dusk or wedding destroyed. Come alone."*

## The Confrontation (Sessions 11–12)

At [Tyr Moir](/places/tyr-moir) graveyard:

1. [Helga](/characters/helga) arrived armed with a hammer, placed her lantern on the central statue
2. [Voris](/characters/voris) **shot the lantern** with an arrow, plunging the graveyard into darkness
3. [Peren](/characters/peren) appeared and vanished among the gravestones like a shadow
4. [Voris](/characters/voris) **rappelled from a rooftop** in a dramatic entrance
5. [Fenris](/characters/fenris) stepped forward at the gate, smoking a pipe

The interrogation revealed crucial information about the [Crown Prince murder](/events/crown-prince-murder) conspiracy:

- A lieutenant filed a false report, influenced by his wife's debts and the [Humanus Association](/factions/humanus-association)
- **Bleiment Skiborg** was named as a connected figure
- Helga promised to **reduce their wanted status** but could never help them openly
- She warned: if they contacted her again, duty would force her to arrest them

A guard Helga had posted was subdued, bound, gagged, and hidden by the party.

## Aftermath

The party left Dior with key intelligence, heading in different directions:
- [Voris](/characters/voris) and [Peren](/characters/peren) rode toward [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep)
- [Fenris](/characters/fenris) and [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) rode toward the smoke from [Sire](/places/sire)
