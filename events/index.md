# Key Events

A timeline of the major events that have shaped our campaign.

<div class="wiki-timeline">
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Crown Prince Murder](/events/crown-prince-murder)
The party is arrested for the murder of the Crown Prince of [Morgan](/places/kingdom-of-morgan) — a crime they did not commit. *Session 1*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Escape from Morgan's Keep](/events/escape-from-morgans-keep)
The party breaks free from captivity and flees the capital, becoming fugitives. *Session 1*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Mercenary Ambush](/events/mercenary-ambush)
[Fat Hans](/characters/fat-hans) and his mercenaries ambush the party on the road, hired by a vengeful countess. *Session 2*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Azure Oak Quest](/events/azure-oak-quest)
In [Turn](/places/turn), the party ventures into an enchanted forest to find a valuable Azure Oak seed. *Sessions 3–4*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [The Crabs Job](/events/the-crabs-job)
The party works with [The Crabs](/factions/the-crabs) gang in [Rinden](/places/rinden) to gain information. *Session 5*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Fey Portal & Flint's Arm](/events/fey-portal)
[Flint](/characters/flint) loses his arm to a closing fey portal as the party leaves the [Fey Realm](/places/fey-realm). *Session 9*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Hooves Bay & Ancient Outpost](/events/hooves-bay-outpost)
The party discovers a hidden ancient elven military outpost near [Hooves Bay](/places/hooves-bay). *Session 9*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Dior Crypt Expedition](/events/dior-crypt)
Guided by [Carlos](/characters/carlos), the party explores a 700-year-old dwarven crypt beneath [Dior](/places/dior), facing traps, goblins, and a dragon. *Sessions 6–8*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Dior Wedding & Helga Confrontation](/events/dior-wedding)
The party schemes around a grand wedding in [Dior](/places/dior), ultimately confronting [Helga](/characters/helga) at [Tyr Moir](/places/tyr-moir) graveyard. *Sessions 10–12*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Burning of Sire](/events/burning-of-sire)
The town of [Sire](/places/sire) burns in a devastating fire; [Fenris](/characters/fenris) and [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) fight a pyromancer amid the flames. *Session 13*

</div>
</div>
