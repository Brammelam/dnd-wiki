# Key Events

A timeline of the major events that have shaped our campaign.

<div class="wiki-timeline">
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Crown Prince Murder](/events/crown-prince-murder)
The party is arrested for the murder of the Crown Prince of [Morgan](/places/kingdom-of-morgan) — a crime they did not commit. *Session 1*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Escape from Morgan's Keep](/events/escape-from-morgans-keep)
The party breaks free from captivity and flees the capital, becoming fugitives. *Session 1*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Mercenary Ambush](/events/mercenary-ambush)
[Fat Hans](/characters/fat-hans) and his mercenaries ambush the party on the road, hired by a vengeful countess. *Session 2*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Azure Oak Quest](/events/azure-oak-quest)
In [Turn](/places/turn), the party ventures into an enchanted forest to find a valuable Azure Oak seed. *Sessions 3–4*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [The Crabs Job](/events/the-crabs-job)
The party works with [The Crabs](/factions/the-crabs) gang in [Rinden](/places/rinden) to gain information. *Session 5*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Scully Stronghold Infiltration](/events/scully-stronghold-infiltration)
The party infiltrates a noble island fortress off [Rinden](/places/rinden) using invisibility, Wild Shape, and a mage's invitation. *Session 6*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Rinden Mud Street Brawl](/events/rinden-mud-street-brawl)
After refusing an assassination contract, [Voris](/characters/voris), [Flint](/characters/flint), and [Peren](/characters/peren) brawl through Mud Street for a dwarf fixer's tenants. *Session 7*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Toltec Box Courier Contract](/events/toltec-box-courier)
[Voris](/characters/voris) and [Elowen](/characters/elowen) leave [Rinden](/places/rinden) toward [Dior](/places/dior) carrying a locked Academy box for [Toltec](/characters/toltec). *Session 8*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Thorn's Serpent and Fey Visions](/events/thorn-serpent-and-visions)
Poison training with [Thorn](/characters/thorn-alchemist), a sea-serpent hunt, and [Elowen](/characters/elowen)'s fey visions near [Havfika](/places/havfika). *Session 9*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Fey Portal & Flint's Arm](/events/fey-portal)
[Flint](/characters/flint) loses his arm to a closing fey portal as the party leaves the [Fey Realm](/places/fey-realm). *Session 10*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Hooves Bay & Ancient Outpost](/events/hooves-bay-outpost)
The party discovers a hidden ancient elven military outpost near [Hooves Bay](/places/hooves-bay). *Session 10*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Lake Hydra Hunt](/events/lake-hydra-hunt)
[Voris](/characters/voris), [Fenris](/characters/fenris), and [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) slay a regenerating hydra in an underground lake; [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) trades with the [Glassmaker](/characters/glassmaker-fey). *Session 11*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Fort John Rebellion](/events/fort-john-rebellion)
Siege, portal seal, and sabotage at [Fort John](/places/fort-john); the party leaves for [Dior](/places/dior) as political sparks fly. *Sessions 12–14*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Dior Crypt Expedition](/events/dior-crypt)
Guided by [Carlos](/characters/carlos), the party explores a 700-year-old dwarven crypt beneath [Dior](/places/dior), facing traps, goblins, and a dragon. *Sessions 16–18*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Dior Wedding & Helga Confrontation](/events/dior-wedding)
The party schemes around a grand wedding in [Dior](/places/dior), ultimately confronting [Helga](/characters/helga) at [Tyr Moir](/places/tyr-moir) graveyard. *Sessions 19–21*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Burning of Sire](/events/burning-of-sire)
The town of [Sire](/places/sire) burns in a devastating fire; [Fenris](/characters/fenris) and [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) fight a pyromancer amid the flames. *Session 22*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Road Inn Ambush](/events/road-inn-ambush)
[Thorn](/characters/thorn-alchemist) drugs and ambushes [Peren](/characters/peren) in a stable, avenging the smashed door and oar trick from the serpent quest; [Voris](/characters/voris) stabilizes Thorn afterward. *Session 23*

</div>
<div class="wiki-timeline-entry">

### [Return to Morgan's Keep](/events/return-to-morgans-keep)
[Voris](/characters/voris) and [Fenris](/characters/fenris) escort survivors to the capital outskirts, reunite with [Thor](/characters/thor-veteran), and open a spymaster path toward the [Crypt of Alabaster](/places/crypt-of-alabaster). *Session 24*

</div>
</div>
