The **murder of the Crown Prince** of the [Kingdom of Morgan](/places/kingdom-of-morgan) is the inciting incident of the entire campaign. The party was falsely accused of this crime and has been fugitives ever since.

## What Happened

The Crown Prince of the Kingdom of Morgan was killed in the capital city of [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep). The circumstances of the actual murder remain unclear to the party, but they were arrested and accused of the crime by the [King's Guard](/factions/kings-guard), led by Captain [Helga](/characters/helga).

## The Frame-Up

Key details about the conspiracy have emerged over the course of the campaign:

- **False confessions** — Under torture, innocent elves confessed to the murder and were executed (revealed by [Helga](/characters/helga))
- **[Lieutenant Jet](/characters/lieutenant-jet)** — A lieutenant in the [King's Guard](/factions/kings-guard) who filed the false report against the party. He was manipulated through his wife's gambling debts and pressure from the [Humanus Association](/factions/humanus-association). His wife is now imprisoned in "the Hole"
- **The Cardinal** — A cardinal in the king's council has the power to compel truth and may be key to uncovering the real culprits
- **[Humanus Association](/factions/humanus-association)** — This human supremacist organization is suspected of orchestrating the frame-up, headquartered in Morgan's Keep

## Consequences

- The party [escaped from Morgan's Keep](/events/escape-from-morgans-keep) and became fugitives
- During the escape, they killed wall guards who were sons of a countess, leading to [mercenary pursuit](/events/mercenary-ambush)
- Wanted posters with their descriptions were distributed across the kingdom (no bounty listed)
- In [Greenfield](/places/greenfield), the party heard that the "guilty" had already been caught and would be hanged — meaning someone else was being blamed
- [Helga](/characters/helga) later promised to reduce their wanted status if they kept a low profile

## Investigation Threads

The party has pursued several leads:
1. **[The Crabs](/factions/the-crabs)** in [Rinden](/places/rinden) — [Salander](/characters/salander) agreed to investigate in exchange for exposing [Father Terry](/characters/father-terry)
2. **[Helga](/characters/helga)** — Provided key information at the [Tyr Moir](/places/tyr-moir) graveyard
3. **Return to Morgan's Keep** — The party is currently heading back to the capital to continue the investigation
4. **The Dragon's task** — The dragon in [the Crypt](/places/the-crypt) asked them to light a rune and explore a hidden place in Morgan's Keep
