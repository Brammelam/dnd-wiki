The infiltration threaded **capital cathedral corridors**, mistakenly roused **Gregory I**, then centred on **[Gregory IV](/characters/gregory-alabaster)**. [Peren](/characters/peren) **wedged a failing door**, taking **many blade thrusts through the gap** (**survived on a sliver of health**). [Varis](/characters/varis) **smashed** the blue corpse-orb **without ending hostilities**, then used **[Amulet of the Living](/characters/varis)** in a **_Turn Undead_-like burst** (**one of three charges spent**). Fenris, ejected earlier after mishaps while wielding **his Blade of the Ethereal** (**monk bump**), re-entered by **lawful means** and followed [Varis](/characters/varis)' scratched directions underground, anchoring **_Wind Wall_** amid flying bone shards (session notes mistranslated that moment briefly; **no stray treasure** recovered from it).

Immediate upstairs fallout—including **[Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok)** amid nave horror—documents under [Capital Cathedral Undead Riot](/events/capital-cathedral-undead-riot) ([Session 26](/sessions/session-26)).
