## Summary

The **capitol congregation** turned **battleground** when tomb-sealed dead—**including the [Crown Prince](/events/crown-prince-murder)**—surfaced after Gregory's **binder orb** shattered in [Session 25](/sessions/session-25). [Fenris](/characters/fenris) **Wind Wall** blackout, **Blade of Ethereal** phasing, and **storm magic** bought inches; [Varis](/characters/varis) hauled [Peren](/characters/peren); [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) **redirected guards**; **[Jamaris Jamarisen Llamar](/characters/jamaris-jamarisen-llamar)** **froze the tide** with counterfeit liturgy long enough to sprint into the [Common District](/places/common-district).
