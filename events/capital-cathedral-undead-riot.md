## Summary

The **capitol congregation** turned **battleground** when tomb-sealed dead—**including the [Crown Prince](/events/crown-prince-murder)**—surfaced after Gregory's **binder orb** shattered in [Session 25](/sessions/session-25). On crypt stairs **[Varis](/characters/varis) carried [Peren](/characters/peren)**, swung the **Amulet of the Living**, and **pinned a shambler** so **only one luminous eye guttered before the door sealed**; a ram **hammered Fenris**, **gangrenous sores** erupted **covering him in bile**, then **Blade of the Ethereal** pulled him and **one attacker** briefly **Ethereal** (**block**, **knock prone**, **stomp**, **return**) before nave carnage resumed. **[Fenris](/characters/fenris) _Wind Wall_** blackout, further **blade** jumps, **call lightning**, and panic bought inches; **[Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok)** **redirected guards**; **[Jamaris Jamarisen Llamar](/characters/jamaris-jamarisen-llamar)** **froze the tide** with counterfeit liturgy long enough to sprint into the [Common District](/places/common-district).
