The **Toltec box contract** began when [Brad](/characters/brad-academy-student), an Academy student, paid [Voris](/characters/voris) and [Elowen](/characters/elowen) **40 gold** to deliver a **locked metal box** to [Professor Toltec](/characters/toltec) in [Dior](/places/dior). The same pair had traveled north after unrest at the gates and a reunion with **[Markus](/characters/markus-lieutenant)**, a guard who knew Voris from military service and who mentioned trouble at [Fort John](/places/fort-john).

## Journey

They haggled for gear, used **detect magic** on market goods, donated to refugees at the walls, and carried the sealed box toward the capital arc in Dior.
