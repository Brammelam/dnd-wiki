| | |
|---|---|

| **Session** | [Session 11](/sessions/session-11) |

The **lake hydra fight** reunited a split party after fey travel. [Voris](/characters/voris) and [Fenris](/characters/fenris) battled a **multi-headed hydra** in a flooded cavern — Fenris used crocodile and **giant octopus** forms while Voris fought with rapier and poisoned arrows. The hydra **regrew heads** until killing blows landed; Fenris was dragged underwater before the creature fell.

[Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok), separated in the forest, met the **[Glassmaker](/characters/glassmaker-fey)** and traded iron gear for a **glass axe** and glasses; [Sommerhimmel](/characters/sommerhimmel) guided her back toward the others. The glass axe **shattered** taking a hydra head.
