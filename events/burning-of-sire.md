The **Burning of Sire** was a devastating fire that consumed the town of [Sire](/places/sire), combining a spreading wildfire with deliberate arson by a pyromancer.

## The Journey

Smoke was visible on the horizon from outside [Dior](/places/dior). [Fenris](/characters/fenris) and [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) rode toward it while [Voris](/characters/voris) and [Peren](/characters/peren) continued to [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep). They rode for a day and a half, passing refugees:
- Mothers with children
- Elderly on donkeys
- Men carrying salvaged possessions
- A panicked man galloping in the opposite direction

The forest across the river was burning, and the fire had jumped to structures near the tree line.

## The Rescue

A young girl came running to them, screaming that her father [Knut Pilsen](/characters/knut-pilsen) was trapped under a burning tree. They rode through pockets of fire:

- [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) **smothered flames with her cloak**, despite her personal **trauma around fire**
- [Fenris](/characters/fenris) carried water from the river using magic
- [Fenris](/characters/fenris) **transformed into a bear** to lift the burning tree
- They carried Knut to the river's edge
- [Fenris](/characters/fenris) healed him with magic
- The daughter climbed onto their horse; Knut's horse Betty had fled across the river

## The Pyromancer

In the town square, they encountered a **scarred man holding a book**, deliberately creating fire with magic. Swirling smoke formed unnatural patterns around him.

The battle was intense:
1. [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) charged in, **severing his hand** with her glass axe — the hand that held the book
2. The pyromancer retaliated with a point-blank fire blast, sending Gal'Rok flying
3. [Fenris](/characters/fenris) crossed a collapsing, burning bridge to reach the fight
4. Fenris **transformed into a giant constricting snake** to grapple the pyromancer and prevent verbal casting
5. [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) landed a devastating axe strike, shattering magical shards
6. The pyromancer gathered himself for a final **point-blank explosion**
7. Both [Fenris](/characters/fenris) and [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) were **knocked unconscious**
8. The pyromancer died in his own explosion

## Evidence Collected

- **Two rapiers** — Salvaged from a smith's shop by Gal'Rok before the building collapsed
