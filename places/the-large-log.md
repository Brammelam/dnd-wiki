**The Large Log** is an inn in [Turn](/places/turn) where the party stayed during their time in the trading town. It was here that they first encountered the local atmosphere — soldiers, a nobleman, and a female orc with a mysterious metal wound — and began planning the [Azure Oak quest](/events/azure-oak-quest).
