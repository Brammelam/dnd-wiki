**Hooves Bay** is a coastal town the party visited after leaving the [Fey Realm](/places/fey-realm).

## Events

Upon arrival, a boy asked the party for help dealing with **pirates** threatening the town. However, the party found the city guards inactive and unwilling to act, so they **chose to leave** rather than get involved.

Later that night, [Sommerhimmel](/characters/sommerhimmel) sent [Elowen](/characters/elowen) on a quest that led the party to discover a hidden **ancient elven military outpost** in a grove near the town. See: [Hooves Bay & Ancient Outpost](/events/hooves-bay-outpost).
