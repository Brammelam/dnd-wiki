**Rinden** is a major port city in the [Kingdom of Morgan](/places/kingdom-of-morgan). It is a hub of trade, crime, and political intrigue.

## Districts

- **[Saltside](/places/saltside)** — The wealthy district where the party sold the Azure Oak seed
- **The Docks** — Northern and Southern, serving river and sea trade
- **The Observatory** — A white tower controlling both sides of the quay

## Key Locations

- **[Toll Guild](/factions/toll-guild) HQ** — Where the guild collects dues and manages trade
- **Fight Pits** — Underground fighting rings where [The Crabs](/factions/the-crabs) operate
- **[The Academy](/factions/the-academy) Office** — A representative offered the party services instead of gold for the Azure Oak seed

## Events

- **[Azure Oak Seed Sale](/events/azure-oak-quest)** — [Elowen](/characters/elowen) sold the seed for 120 gold in the Saltside district
- **[The Crabs Job](/events/the-crabs-job)** — [Peren](/characters/peren) and [Voris](/characters/voris) infiltrated the gang to gain intelligence

## Description

Rinden is a bustling port city divided by economic class. The wealthy [Saltside](/places/saltside) district features exclusive clubs and high society, while the seedier areas host criminal gangs like [The Crabs](/factions/the-crabs). The [Toll Guild](/factions/toll-guild) exerts significant economic control over trade.

When the party arrived, rumors were spreading that the Crown Prince was dead and the "guilty" had been caught and would be hanged — contradicting what the party knew, since they had escaped and were supposed to be the suspects.
