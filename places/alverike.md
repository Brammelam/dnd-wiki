**Alverike** (the Elven Realm) is the homeland of the elves, renowned for its exceptional metalwork and rare craftsmanship — including the production of optical **glasses** that are virtually unknown in the rest of the [Kingdom of Morgan](/places/kingdom-of-morgan).

## Notable Exports

- **Magical glasses** — Rare optical aids that can correct vision. The party carried a pair that [Fenris](/characters/fenris) sold to [Gilda](/characters/gilda) at [the Academy](/places/the-academy) for 300 gold. [Voris](/characters/voris) also uses a pair for his farsightedness.
- **Fine metalwork** — Alverike is known for quality metal craftsmanship

The glasses were described as being made by a "glass master in Alverike," and few people in the wider world understood their function as optical aids.
