**Mountain Dew's Rest** is a modest inn in [Dior](/places/dior) run by a dwarf bartender who polishes glasses behind the bar. The party chose it as their base during their extended stay in the mountain city.

The inn provided rooms with fresh meals included in the stay. It was from here that the party planned their crypt expedition, wedding infiltration, and confrontation with [Helga](/characters/helga).
