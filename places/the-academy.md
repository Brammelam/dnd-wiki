**The Academy** is a scholarly institution located on the morning side of [Dior](/places/dior). Despite its name, the building resembles a **library or bookshop** more than a school, with shelves full of books and a reception desk.

## Description

The Academy is housed in a modest building with a small tower on top. Inside, it is filled with books on various subjects (the party noticed one on evocation). [Gilda](/characters/gilda) works the reception desk, controlling access to senior academics like [Toltec](/characters/toltec).

The upper floors contain offices with slanted roofs and stained glass windows, where [Toltec](/characters/toltec) received the party's delivery.

## Role in the Story

The party had a mission to deliver a **mysterious box** to [Toltec](/characters/toltec) at the Academy. This delivery was the main reason they traveled to [Dior](/places/dior). The negotiations at the Academy also involved selling [Alverike](/places/alverike) glasses to [Gilda](/characters/gilda) for 300 gold.

The Academy also has a presence in [Rinden](/places/rinden), where a representative once offered the party services instead of gold for the Azure Oak seed.
