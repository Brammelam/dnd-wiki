**Dior** is a mountain city built inside a circular valley, framed by mountains on all sides. It is one of the most significant locations in the campaign, where the party spent several sessions exploring, shopping, and scheming.

## Description

The city is accessed through a steep, winding mountain road that leads to a **large tunnel** flanked by two impressive statues. The tunnel is well-lit and busy with travelers, beggars, and guards. Emerging from the tunnel reveals a **circular valley** with an artificial lake at its center, surrounded by buildings rising up the mountain slopes.

In the middle of the lake stands a **great temple** that extends over the water. The city is divided into a **morning side** (east, catching sunrise) and an **evening side** (west), with strict building regulations preventing any structure from rising above the surrounding mountains.

There is also a distinct **shadow side** — a darker, seedier part of the city where [Carlos](/characters/carlos) operates and where less legitimate business takes place.

## Key Locations

- **The Temple** — A massive structure in the lake, used for weddings and worship; has multiple floors including a basement partially under water
- **[The Academy](/places/the-academy)** — A scholarly institution on the morning side

- **[Bob Swords](/characters/bob-swords)' Shop** — Weapon and armor dealer
- **[Tore Idle](/characters/tore-idle)'s Workshop** — Dragonborn smith
- **Nymph's Tears** — A curiosity shop selling potions (laxatives, sleep aids, cosmetics)
- **The Shadow Side** — Where [Carlos](/characters/carlos) operates from a cantina-style bar
- **[The Crypt](/places/the-crypt)** — Ancient dwarven burial site beneath the city
- **[Tyr Moir](/places/tyr-moir)** — Graveyard outside the city
- **Griselda's "The Blue Cloak"** — A clothing rental shop

## Notable Details

- The city has a **guild registration system** requiring identity cards
- **Wanted posters** with the party's descriptions exist but have no bounty
- Building codes prevent structures from exceeding the mountain height
- Three Dragonborn live in the city: [Toltec](/characters/toltec) (dark green, academic), [Tore Idle](/characters/tore-idle) (smith), and a white one (bow seller)
