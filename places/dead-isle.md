**Dead Isle** is a mysterious location accessible only through a **sealed portal** discovered in the [ancient elven military outpost](/events/hooves-bay-outpost) near [Hooves Bay](/places/hooves-bay).

The party found the portal alongside **crystals** by an underground lake. When they took the largest crystal, [Elowen](/characters/elowen) had a vision of a **masked woman** who had once protected the place. Movement was detected in the lake after the crystal was touched, but the party chose not to investigate further.

The Dead Isle remains unexplored and its nature unknown.
