**Scully Stronghold** is a noble redoubt on an island visible from [Rinden](/places/rinden)'s harbor. Passage is controlled by a **lektør** barge; boarding typically requires an invitation from **Lady Amber**.

## Layout (observed)

A dock, outbuildings, and a path toward a **main gate** — solid walls and a small tower, guarded but not overwhelming.
