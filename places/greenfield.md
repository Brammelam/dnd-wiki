**Greenfield** is a small town the party passed through on their way from [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep) to [Rinden](/places/rinden) after the [escape](/events/escape-from-morgans-keep).

## Events

In Greenfield, the party heard a disturbing rumor: the Crown Prince was dead, and the "guilty" had already been caught and were to be hanged. This contradicted reality — the party had just escaped, and they were the ones who had been accused. The inconsistency suggested that **someone else** had been arrested (or executed) in their place, deepening the mystery of the [Crown Prince murder](/events/crown-prince-murder) conspiracy.
