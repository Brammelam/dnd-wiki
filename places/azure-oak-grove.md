**The Azure Oak Grove** is an enchanted clearing in the forest near [Turn](/places/turn), home to fey creatures and the magical Azure Oak tree.

## Description

Deep in the forest near Turn, the grove is protected by **thorns** and guarded by fey creatures. The Azure Oak itself is a large, magical tree whose seeds are extremely valuable — worth enough to feed a drought-stricken town.

## The Quest

[Practical Joe](/characters/practical-joe) told the party that an Azure Oak seed could pay for food during [Turn](/places/turn)'s drought. The party ventured into the forest and encountered fey magic:

- [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) was **lured** by fairy charm and chased a fairy toward a ravine
- [Elowen](/characters/elowen) received a **patron Calling** from her warlock powers
- The party found the Azure Oak surrounded by thorns
- [Fenris](/characters/fenris), in **squirrel form**, climbed to the top of the tree
- Disturbing the fairies caused them to merge into **one large fairy** that attacked, knocking Fenris down and threatening the party with roots
- After defeating the hostile fairies, the thorns wilted, and a **small blue fairy** from the Azure Oak permitted them to take a single seed

The seed was later sold for 120 gold in [Rinden](/places/rinden)'s [Saltside](/places/saltside) district.
