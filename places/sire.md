**Sire** is a town along a river that was devastated by fire, partly from a spreading wildfire and partly from a **pyromancer's** deliberate attacks.

## The Burning

Smoke was first visible from outside [Dior](/places/dior), drawing [Fenris](/characters/fenris) and [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) to investigate. They rode for a day and a half, passing refugees fleeing with what little they could carry — mothers with children, elderly on donkeys, and men carrying salvaged possessions.

The forest across the river was burning, and the fire had jumped to buildings near the forest edge. When they arrived, most residents had been evacuated by soldiers, but pockets of fire still raged.

## The Pyromancer

In the town square, they encountered a **burned, scarred man** holding a book, surrounded by swirling smoke. He had been deliberately setting fires with magic. [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) engaged him in brutal melee, severing his hand with her glass axe, while [Fenris](/characters/fenris) transformed into a giant snake to grapple him. The pyromancer fought back with point-blank fire explosions, knocking both unconscious before finally dying.

[Fenris](/characters/fenris) collected a **glowing medallion** and the pyromancer's book pages as evidence. A dead body in the rubble carried a purse with the name **Sigurd**.
