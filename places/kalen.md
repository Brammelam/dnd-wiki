**Kalén** is a settlement along the river, mentioned in connection with the fires spreading from the west. It lies not by the sea but along the river, with a nearby town called **Tørn** and forested areas known as **Nordmarkalén** surrounding it.

Soldiers stationed outside [Dior](/places/dior) mentioned Kalén as being near the fire zone. Communications from the settlement were absent, adding to the anxiety about the spreading blaze that eventually consumed [Sire](/places/sire).
