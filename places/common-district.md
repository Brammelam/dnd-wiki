The **Common District** is Morgan's bustling **southward sprawl**: narrow alleys packed with **beer shops**, improvised **wash houses**, patrol routes, and **refuge tents** bordering the cathedral plaza. [Session 26](/sessions/session-26)'s riot survivors sheltered there while sketching infiltration gambits targeting **[Lady Alabaster](/characters/lady-alabaster)**'s **Humanus-aligned salon**.
