### Fort John

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Fortress / siege site |
| **Region** | North of [Rinden](/places/rinden), near fey-touched wilds |
| **First Seen** | [Session 12](/sessions/session-12) |

</WikiInfobox>

**Fort John** was the seat of a regional crisis when **[Karl Marsensen](/characters/karl-marsensen)**'s arrest sparked a **rebel occupation**. **[Baron Peridott](/characters/baron-peridott)** besieged it with **[Markus](/characters/markus-lieutenant)** among his officers. The party scouted rusted hinges, side doors, and river approaches; internal maps included towers, a **beer cellar**, and divided loyalties among pressed soldiers.
