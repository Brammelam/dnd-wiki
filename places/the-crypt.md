**The Crypt of Dior** (also called **the Klypten**) is a 700-year-old sealed dwarven burial site beneath [Dior](/places/dior). The party explored it over three sessions, guided by [Carlos](/characters/carlos) who had discovered a secret entrance.

## Layout

The crypt is a massive underground complex built into the mountain:

### Entry Level
- **Secret entrance** — Through loose planks in a cellar wall behind the temple, leading to a natural crack in the rock
- **Rune-covered corridors** — Ancient dwarvish inscriptions on the walls
- **Dwarf statues** — Stone figures holding helmets, some with urns containing only dust

### Puzzle Door
A heavy stone door flanked by **four dwarf statues**, each holding a different weapon (hammer, axe, sword, morningstar). The door had no handle and was locked, with inscriptions in Old Dwarvish above it. Each statue had **four buttons** on its clothing. The puzzle was solved by pressing buttons in sequence (first button on first statue, second on second, etc.).

### The Bridge & River
A **10-meter gap** over an underground river with hot, steaming water and jagged rocks below. The party crossed using a rope bridge improvised from a fired arrow, [Fenris](/characters/fenris)'s entangle spell, and 50 feet of climbing rope.

### The Underground City
An enormous cylindrical cavern containing a **dwarven city** built into the walls — houses on both sides of a broad main street, rising in tiers. The air was heavy and visibility poor. **Goblins** had taken up residence, growing mushrooms and occupying the abandoned buildings.

### The Central Platform
A raised platform in the center of the cavern with a **stone sarcophagus** guarded by four dwarf statues bearing weapons. A mushroom patch grew around it. Taking the **knives** from one statue triggered a **fire elemental** that attacked the party.

### The Dragon's Lair
Beneath the platform lurked a **massive dragon** (reddish, fire-breathing) that emerged after the party defeated the fire elemental. A telepathic voice spoke in their heads, offering a deal.

## Encounters

1. **Magical trap** — Magical threads of light tried to pull [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) apart at the puzzle door
2. **Fire Elemental** — Awakened when [Peren](/characters/peren) took knives from a statue; resistant to physical damage
3. **Goblin ambush** — Multiple waves of goblins in the underground city
4. **The Dragon** — Demanded an oath of silence in exchange for treasure and safe passage

## Treasure

- **Cursed rust knives** — Cause stacking bleed damage on hit
- **Ancient dwarven coins** — 19 found in burial urns
- **12 short rusty hammers** — Low value but collected
- **Copper chest** — Massive chest of copper coins worth 583 gold (bought by [Carlos](/characters/carlos))
- **Goblin ears** — 10 collected (turned out to be worthless)

## The Dragon's Deal

The dragon offered the party the treasure chest if they performed a service: travel to [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep), light a special rune with coal, and explore a hidden ancient place there. All party members swore an oath to **never reveal the dragon's existence**, sealed with a magical brand on [Voris](/characters/voris).
