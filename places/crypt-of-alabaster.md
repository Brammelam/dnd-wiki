The **Crypt of Alabaster** lies beneath the Morganian **cathedral**. It surfaced in [Session 24](/sessions/session-24) as the **exoneration** destination: questioning **[Gregory Alabaster](/characters/gregory-alabaster)** with the spymaster’s **limited-use ring**, tying to [Lieutenant Jet](/characters/lieutenant-jet) and the [Crown Prince murder](/events/crown-prince-murder).

[Session 25](/sessions/session-25)'s **[Alabaster Crypt Infiltration](/events/alabaster-crypt-infiltration)** put [Varis](/characters/varis), [Peren](/characters/peren), and [Fenris](/characters/fenris) through **six-branch undercrofts** beneath the nave—shields of noble houses (**[Lady Amber](/characters/lady-amber)**'s **fish** emblem among them)—after a **Gregory I** mis-fire and confrontation with **[Gregory IV](/characters/gregory-alabaster)** carrying a cobalt corpse-orb **[Varis](/characters/varis) smashed without lifting the siege**. **[Amulet of the Living](/characters/varis)** expelled one rush of undead (**one charge spent, two remain**) while [Peren](/characters/peren) held a jammed doorway through multiple stab wounds, and Fenris used **Blade of the Ethereal** timing plus **_Wind Wall_** to break contact (notes garbled **bone splinters** briefly; nothing extra was looted).
