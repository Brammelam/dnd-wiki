**Morgan's Keep** is the capital city of the [Kingdom of Morgan](/places/kingdom-of-morgan). It is where the campaign begins and where most of the party lived before being framed for the [Crown Prince's murder](/events/crown-prince-murder).

## Description

Morgan's Keep is a large, well-fortified city that serves as the seat of the kingdom's power. It houses the royal palace, the great **Tyr** temple of the [Church of the Triad](/factions/temple-of-tyr), and the headquarters of the [King's Guard](/factions/kings-guard). The city has a functioning City Watch and a complex social hierarchy.

## Key Locations

- **The Royal Palace** — Seat of the king; where the Crown Prince was murdered
- **[Church of the Triad](/factions/temple-of-tyr)** — Tyr's great temple in the capital, where [Flint](/characters/flint) served as an acolyte
- **The Prison** — Where the party was held before their escape
- **[Humanus Association](/factions/humanus-association) Headquarters** — Base of the supremacist organization

## Events

- **[Crown Prince Murder](/events/crown-prince-murder)** — The inciting incident; the party was arrested here
- **[Escape from Morgan's Keep](/events/escape-from-morgans-keep)** — The party fought through guards and fled
- **Guard killings** — During the escape, the party killed wall guards who turned out to be sons of a countess, leading to the [mercenary ambush](/events/mercenary-ambush)

## Connections

The party was originally heading to [Rinden](/places/rinden) to investigate the framing. Later sessions revealed that [Helga](/characters/helga) could reduce their wanted status if they stayed low. The [Humanus Association](/factions/humanus-association), headquartered here, is suspected of being behind the framing. The party is currently traveling back toward Morgan's Keep to continue their investigation.
