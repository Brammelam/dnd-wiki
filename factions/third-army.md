# Third Army

### Third Army

| | |
|---|---|

| **Scope** | Kingdom-wide (includes formations such as the **Fourth Brigade**) |

</WikiInfobox>

The **Third Army** is a principal military pillar of the [Kingdom of Morgan](/places/kingdom-of-morgan). It operates alongside (not identical to) the capital-focused [King's Guard](/factions/kings-guard). The **Fourth Brigade** is part of the Third Army.

Military religious practice in the field often invokes the same patrons as the [Church of the Triad](/factions/temple-of-tyr): **Tyr**, **Torm**, and **Ilmater**.
