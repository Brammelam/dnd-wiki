**The King's Guard** is the primary military and law enforcement arm of the crown in the [Kingdom of Morgan](/places/kingdom-of-morgan). They are responsible for maintaining order, enforcing the king's laws, and protecting the realm.

## Role in the Story

The King's Guard arrested the party for the [Crown Prince's murder](/events/crown-prince-murder) in [Session 1](/sessions/session-01). Captain [Helga](/characters/helga) led the operation, and the interrogator [Lynch](/characters/lynch) was tasked with extracting confessions.

After the party's [escape](/events/escape-from-morgans-keep), the Guard maintained wanted posters with the party's descriptions across the kingdom, though notably **without a bounty**. Guards have been spotted asking questions in [Turn](/places/turn) and other towns.

## Known Operations

- **Crown Prince investigation** — Led to the wrongful arrest of the party
- **Executions** — Innocent elves were tortured into confessions and executed for the murder
- **Military mobilization** — A large force was observed camped outside [Dior](/places/dior), preparing for a march

## Corruption

[Helga](/characters/helga) revealed that a **lieutenant** filed the report that led to the party's arrest. This lieutenant was likely influenced by his wife's gambling debts and pressure from the [Humanus Association](/factions/humanus-association). The corruption within the Guard is a central thread of the campaign's mystery.

## Members
