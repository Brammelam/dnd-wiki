**The Crabs** are a criminal gang operating in [Rinden](/places/rinden). They control fight pits and engage in various underground activities. Despite their criminal nature, they proved to be useful allies.

## Role in the Story

[Peren](/characters/peren) and [Voris](/characters/voris) sought out The Crabs in [Session 5](/sessions/session-05) to find information about who framed them for the [Crown Prince's murder](/events/crown-prince-murder). To earn the gang's trust, they:

1. Watched the **fight pits** — underground combat arenas
2. **Saved a Crabs member** from danger
3. [Peren](/characters/peren) received a **poison-making course** from the gang

This earned them a meeting with the leader, [Salander](/characters/salander), who offered a deal: he would investigate the framing if they exposed the shady dealings of [Father Terry](/characters/father-terry).

## Operations

- **Fight pits** — Underground fighting rings for entertainment and gambling
- **Information brokering** — Salander has networks capable of digging into political conspiracies
- **Criminal services** — Various illegal activities in Rinden's underworld
