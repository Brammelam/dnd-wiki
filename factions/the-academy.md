**The Academy** is a kingdom-wide scholarly organization. It is **not** "the Dior school" alone: [Dior](/places/dior) hosts one prominent office in a [dedicated building](/places/the-academy), while other branches operate elsewhere.

## Structure

Senior academics such as [Toltec](/characters/toltec) are difficult to reach without an appointment or a valuable delivery. [Lynn Salt](/characters/lynn-salt) serves as Dior receptionist, courier, and logistics contact for Toltec.

## Operations

- **Research** — Magic, evocation, and other arcane topics
- **Artifact acquisition** — The party delivered a mysterious box to [Toltec](/characters/toltec)
- **Courier network** — Lynn transports magical ingredients and represents the Academy on the road
- **Trade** — The Rinden office offered services (rather than gold) for the Azure Oak seed

## Role in the Story

The party traveled to [Dior](/places/dior) to deliver a sealed box to Toltec; he paid **200 gold** and forbade them from opening it.

Toltec discussed **optical lenses** and vision correction; Lynn purchased [Alverike](/places/alverike) glasses from the party for **300 gold**.
