**The Academy** is a scholarly institution with its main campus in [Dior](/places/dior) and a presence in [Rinden](/places/rinden). It deals in magical knowledge, artifacts, and research.

## Structure

The Academy operates with a hierarchical system where senior academics like [Toltec](/characters/toltec) are difficult to access. [Gilda](/characters/gilda) works as a gatekeeper at the reception desk, and [Frida](/characters/frida) serves as Toltec's personal assistant. Access to powerful individuals requires either an appointment or a delivery of something valuable.

## Operations

- **Research** — Academic study of magic, evocation, and other arcane topics
- **Artifact acquisition** — The party was hired to deliver a mysterious box to [Toltec](/characters/toltec)
- **Courier network** — [Lynn Salt](/characters/lynn-salt) transports magical ingredients for the Academy
- **Trade** — The Rinden office offered the party services (rather than gold) for the Azure Oak seed

## Role in the Story

The Academy was the reason the party traveled to [Dior](/places/dior) — they had a mission to deliver a sealed box to [Toltec](/characters/toltec), who paid 200 gold upon delivery and instructed them never to look inside.

[Toltec](/characters/toltec) mentioned knowledge of **optical lenses** and magical vision correction, and [Gilda](/characters/gilda) purchased [Alverike](/places/alverike) glasses from the party for 300 gold.
