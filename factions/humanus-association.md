**The Humanus Association** is a powerful and influential organization headquartered in [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep). They hold strong **human supremacist** views and prejudices against non-human races.

## Role in the Story

[Helga](/characters/helga) identified the Humanus Association as a key player in the conspiracy behind the [Crown Prince's murder](/events/crown-prince-murder) framing. During the [Tyr Moir confrontation](/events/dior-wedding), she revealed:

- The Association exerted **pressure** on a lieutenant in the [King's Guard](/factions/kings-guard), whose wife had gambling debts
- This pressure likely influenced the false report that led to the party's arrest
- Their headquarters is in [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep)
- They pay well and are suspected of involvement in **several brutal cases**
- They are described as "no friendly gang" — powerful, dangerous, and deeply embedded in the kingdom's power structure

The cardinal in the king's council was mentioned in connection with the Association's influence, suggesting their reach extends to the highest levels of government.

## Threat Level

The Humanus Association represents one of the campaign's primary antagonist factions. Their human supremacist ideology, combined with political connections and willingness to use violence and corruption, makes them extremely dangerous. The party will likely need to confront them directly as they work to clear their names.
