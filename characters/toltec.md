**Toltec** is a dark-green-scaled Dragonborn and a senior academic at [The Academy](/places/the-academy) in [Dior](/places/dior). The party was tasked with delivering a mysterious box to him.

## Description

Toltec is described as having **dark green, faded scales** and an owl-like appearance. He typically wears magical robes. He is one of three Dragonborn in Dior — the others being a white one (a bow seller) and a red one (a monk). He is an important but difficult-to-reach figure within the Academy hierarchy.

## Role in the Story

The party carried a **mysterious box** that they had been tasked to deliver specifically to Toltec at the Academy. [Lynn Salt](/characters/lynn-salt), at reception, explained that Toltec was important but hard to get access to, and arranged a meeting for the next evening at seven.

When the party finally delivered the box, Toltec — described as an old green Dragonborn in an upper-floor office with stained glass windows — paid them **200 gold** for the delivery. He instructed them never to look at the contents. [Lynn Salt](/characters/lynn-salt) handled follow-up paperwork and logistics.

Toltec also mentioned the existence of **magical lenses** for correcting vision — far-sightedness and near-sightedness — though the explanation was too scientific for the party to fully grasp.
