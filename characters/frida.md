**Frida** is [Toltec](/characters/toltec)'s assistant at [The Academy](/places/the-academy) in [Dior](/places/dior). (Not [Frida Marsensen](/characters/frida-marsensen) of [Fort John](/places/fort-john).)

## Role in the Story

After the party delivered the mysterious box to [Toltec](/characters/toltec) and received their 200 gold payment, Frida handled the logistics. [Fenris](/characters/fenris) showed her a sack of strange pollen he had collected, and she suggested it could be used to influence people if he wanted to speak with her further — hinting at knowledge of alchemical or magical uses for fey materials.
