**Father Terry** is a figure of dubious reputation in [Rinden](/places/rinden). [Salander](/characters/salander), leader of [The Crabs](/factions/the-crabs), wants his shady business dealings exposed.

## Role in the Story

Father Terry was mentioned as part of the deal between [Salander](/characters/salander) and the party. In exchange for investigating who framed the party for the [Crown Prince's murder](/events/crown-prince-murder), Salander asked them to expose Father Terry's corrupt activities. The exact nature of his dealings has not yet been revealed.
