**Thia** is a Drow Sorcerer played by **Maria**. Approximately 200 years old, she is uncomfortable with human civilization after spending most of her long life in the woods, though she has grown to enjoy the bustle of city life in [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep).

## Background

Thia spent most of her two centuries living in the forests, away from civilization. As a Drow, she faces prejudice from many who associate her kind with evil. Despite this, she has developed a fondness for big-city life in [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep).

A pivotal moment came when she met another traveling Drow who sensed **old magic** within her — an ancient sorcerous potential waiting to be developed. This discovery excited Thia and drove her to seek out ways to develop her innate magical abilities.

## Adventures

### The Escape (Session 1)
Thia was arrested alongside [Flint](/characters/flint), [Voris](/characters/voris), and [Peren](/characters/peren) for the [Crown Prince's murder](/events/crown-prince-murder). She helped fight through the guards and escape [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep), traveling with the group toward [Rinden](/places/rinden).
