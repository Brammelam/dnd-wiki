**Carlos** is a Half-Orc treasure hunter and amateur archaeologist from **warmer northern lands** (his name reflects that heritage). He is based on the shadow side of [Dior](/places/dior) and guided the party into [the Crypt of Dior](/places/the-crypt) through a secret entrance he had discovered.

## Description

Carlos operates from a bar on the shadow side of Dior — a dimly lit establishment with a half-orc drinking wine at the bar and a dwarf polishing glasses. The atmosphere reminded the party of a classic Mexican cantina. Carlos speaks warmly and was pleased when [Peren](/characters/peren) greeted him in Spanish.

## Role in the Story

### The Crypt Deal
Carlos had discovered an **alternative entrance** to [the Crypt](/places/the-crypt), a 700-year-old sealed dwarven burial site that no one had entered in centuries. The entrance was through loose planks in a cellar wall, leading to a natural crack in the rock. He offered the party a deal:

- He would guide them to the entrance and wait outside
- They would explore the crypt and retrieve treasures
- He would take **25% of the loot** plus a commission on selling the finds
- His bad knee prevented him from going inside himself

He warned them that the dwarves who built the crypt were known for **deadly traps**.

### The Aftermath
When the party emerged with ancient copper coins, rusty knives, and dwarven gold, Carlos inspected the haul and offered **583 gold** for the chest of copper coins. He handled the fencing of the goods and divided the payment fairly.
