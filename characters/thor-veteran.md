**Thor** first met [Voris](/characters/voris) and [Peren](/characters/peren) on the road toward [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep), sharing cheese and rumors of **Fortrejon** and **Kopp**.

In the capital outskirts ([Session 24](/sessions/session-24)), [Voris](/characters/voris) found him changed — **battle-worn**, with a **staff** and **bad leg**. Thor's vouch mattered to a **spymaster** offering help. He returned with **[Magda](/characters/magda-artificer)**.
