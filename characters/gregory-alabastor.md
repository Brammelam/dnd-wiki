**Gregory Alabastor** is the **recently deceased** man the party’s exoneration plan hinges on: they intend to **reach him in the [Crypt of Alabaster](/places/crypt-of-alabaster)** (with the spymaster’s **limited-use ring** to question the dead) to advance clearing their names in the [Crown Prince murder](/events/crown-prince-murder) affair.

Surname ties him to the **Alabaster** great house ([Kingdom of Morgan § Politics](/places/kingdom-of-morgan#politics)); details of his life and death are to be established in play.
