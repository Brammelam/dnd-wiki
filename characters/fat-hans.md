**Fat Hans** is a former guard turned mercenary who led a squad to ambush the party on the road.

## Role in the Story

After a cart crash, Fat Hans and his mercenaries caught up to the party. He revealed that the guards the party had killed while [escaping Morgan's Keep](/events/escape-from-morgans-keep) were the **sons of a countess**, who had hired the mercenaries to exact revenge.

The party defeated the mercenaries in combat, stole a horse, a compass, and gear. They tied Hans and his surviving men to a tree before moving on.

See: [Mercenary Ambush](/events/mercenary-ambush)

## Relationships

- **The Countess** — His employer, seeking revenge for her sons
