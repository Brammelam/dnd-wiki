**Mirbog** guarded a **sealed portal** on behalf of **Titania**, anchored by three relics — shield, sword, and mask. [Elowen](/characters/elowen) claimed the **mask**, strengthening illusions. Mirbog provided a **formula to reseal** the gate if the dead horde beyond were ever unleashed.
