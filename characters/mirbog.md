**Mirbog** is a Half-orc **portal warden** for **Titania**, tending **another knot** along the same undeath-aligned fault strained when **[Fenris](/characters/fenris)** stole **Blade of the Ethereal** and **[Elowen](/characters/elowen)** claimed **Sprout** + **Nathair's Mask** from the **[Lake Hydra Hunt](/events/lake-hydra-hunt)** crystals ([Session 12](/sessions/session-12)). Encounter prose often maps those relic metaphors onto **blade / shield / mask** imagery; Mirbog **did not newly award** **Nathair's Mask**, but keyed his ritual through Elowen's **existing bond** to it. He taught a **resealing formula** in case planar rot ever broke through outright.
