**Thorn** is a reclusive poisoner recommended by [Griselda](/characters/griselda-mud-street). He trained [Elowen](/characters/elowen) in recipes including **Dobbelsvin** and sent the party to harvest **sea serpent** venom on an island.

## The oars and the door

To force **[Peren](/characters/peren)** to face the serpent **without help**, Thorn **coated the rowboat oars** with **sleeping potion** so several companions **collapsed** when they reached the island. [Elowen](/characters/elowen) responded with a **rage** and **destroyed his front door**.

## Road revenge

Later, Thorn tracked [Voris](/characters/voris) and Peren on the road ([Road Inn Ambush](/events/road-inn-ambush)): disguised as a **traveling herbalist**, he **drugged their drinks**, then **ambushed Peren alone** in the stable. [Voris](/characters/voris) helped subdue him and used a **healing potion** to **stabilize** Thorn after the fight. Peren kept Thorn's **poisoning notebook**.
