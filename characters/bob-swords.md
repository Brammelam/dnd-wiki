**Bob Swords** is a jovial Hobbit weapon merchant in [Dior](/places/dior). Despite his small stature, he deals in powerful magical weapons and armor, and has become a reliable ally and information source for the party.

## Inventory

Bob's shop features a variety of magical and mundane equipment:

- **Frost Leather Vest** — Armor from the cold north that protects the wearer and deals cold damage to attackers, but risks harming the wearer as well
- **Standard Leather Vest** — Better protection without magical side effects, at a lower cost
- **Runed Armor** — Expensive armor with magical runes providing 30 seconds of damage protection, with severe backlash afterward
- **Elemental Arrows** — Fire, ice, and lightning arrows (100 gold per arrow)
- **Grappling Hook** — Standard climbing equipment

Bob confirmed that magical armor would **adapt to a shapeshifter's form**, easing [Fenris](/characters/fenris)'s concerns about Wild Shape compatibility.

## Role in the Story

Bob connected the party with [Carlos](/characters/carlos) for the crypt expedition and later provided tactical support during the wedding plot. He helped procure **wedding invitations** and offered to work behind the scenes. He also promised to research the temple's blueprints — wall thickness, drainage systems, and interior layout — for the party's infiltration plans.
