**Sommerhimmel** (meaning "Summer Sky" in Norwegian) is a fey entity encountered in the [Fey Realm](/places/fey-realm).

## Role in the Story

Sommerhimmel was angry at [Elowen](/characters/elowen) for **lying to the fey**. Rather than punishing her outright, Sommerhimmel sent Elowen on a task: to find a **hidden treasure** in a grove. This led the party to discover a secret phrase that opened an **ancient elven military outpost** near [Hooves Bay](/places/hooves-bay).

The encounter demonstrated that the fey take honesty seriously and that deception in the [Fey Realm](/places/fey-realm) has consequences — though sometimes those consequences lead to unexpected rewards.
