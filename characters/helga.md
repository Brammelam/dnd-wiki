**Helga** is a captain of the [King's Guard](/factions/kings-guard) who originally arrested the party for the [Crown Prince's murder](/events/crown-prince-murder). She later reappeared in [Dior](/places/dior) during wedding preparations, where the party confronted her seeking answers.

## Description

Helga is a commanding woman with blonde hair, typically seen in the polished, practical armor of the King's Guard. She carries a heavy hammer on her back and moves with **military discipline**. She commands respect from soldiers and servants alike, giving rapid, authoritative instructions.

## Role in the Story

### The Arrest
Helga was responsible for arresting the party at [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep), accusing them of the [Crown Prince's murder](/events/crown-prince-murder). Under her supervision, innocent elves were tortured into false confessions and subsequently executed.

### Dior Wedding
Helga appeared in [Dior](/places/dior) overseeing preparations for a grand wedding at the temple. She was surrounded by guards and acted in an administrative capacity, directing workers, priests, and soldiers. The party spent days surveilling her movements, learning her routines, and planning their confrontation.

### The Graveyard Confrontation
The party lured Helga to the [Tyr Moir](/places/tyr-moir) graveyard with an anonymous threatening message: *"Meet at Tyr Moir, at dusk or wedding destroyed. Come alone."* She came alone but armed, setting her lantern on a statue of Tyr Moir.

[Voris](/characters/voris) shot out the lantern, plunging the graveyard into darkness. After a tense standoff, Helga revealed key information:
- She still suspected the party but was **willing to help** within limits
- Her position in the King's Guard bound her; she couldn't act openly
- A **lieutenant** had filed the damning report, likely influenced by his wife's gambling debts and pressure from the [Humanus Association](/factions/humanus-association)
- She named **Bleiment Skiborg** as a person of interest
- She promised to **reduce their wanted status** if they kept a low profile
- She warned that if they contacted her again, her duty would force her to arrest them
