**Gilda** is a Dwarven woman in her early sixties who works as the receptionist at [The Academy](/places/the-academy) in [Dior](/places/dior). She has curly hair and visible vision problems, often squinting while reading.

## Role in the Story

When the party arrived at the Academy to deliver a box to [Toltec](/characters/toltec), Gilda was the gatekeeper. [Fenris](/characters/fenris) offered her a pair of magical glasses from [Alverike](/places/alverike), but they didn't suit her vision. She was still interested in buying them and, after hard negotiation, purchased them for **300 gold** — having initially tried to lowball at 200.

Gilda controlled access to Toltec and was suspected by the party of being somewhat manipulative. She arranged a meeting time for the following evening at seven.
