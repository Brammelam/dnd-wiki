**Knut Pilsen** is a resident of [Sire](/places/sire) who was rescued during the [Burning of Sire](/events/burning-of-sire).

## Role in the Story

During the devastating fire that consumed [Sire](/places/sire), Knut's daughter came running to [Fenris](/characters/fenris) and [Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok), screaming that her father was trapped and would burn to death. The two heroes rushed to find Knut pinned under a fallen, burning tree near the riverbank, his hair on fire.

[Gal'Rok](/characters/galrok) smothered the flames with her cloak while [Fenris](/characters/fenris) fetched water from the river using magic. Fenris then transformed into a bear to lift the tree, and together they carried Knut to safety at the river's edge. Fenris used healing magic to stabilize him.

Once revived, Knut called out for **Betty** — his horse, which had fled across the river. His daughter had climbed onto the party's horse during the rescue.
