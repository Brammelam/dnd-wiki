**Voris** is a Half-Elf Fighter played by **Bram**. A veteran of the Morganian army, Voris served as a swordsman before developing farsightedness that forced him to retrain as an archer. He has since become a formidable samurai-style bowman.

## Background

Voris served in the Morganian army as a swordsman. Over time, his vision deteriorated — he became farsighted, making close-quarters combat increasingly difficult. Rather than retire, he adapted, retraining himself as an archer where his long-distance vision became an asset rather than a liability.

He was living in [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep) when he was arrested alongside other party members for the [murder of the Crown Prince](/events/crown-prince-murder).

## Notable Equipment

- **Longbow** — His primary weapon, repaired and upgraded by [Tore Idle](/characters/tore-idle) in [Dior](/places/dior). He chose a more powerful variant with longer range at the cost of aiming difficulty.
- **Rapier** — Secondary melee weapon
- **Glasses** — Rare optical aids from [Alverike](/places/alverike); he sold a pair to [Gilda](/characters/gilda) for 300 gold
- **City Watch insignia** — A badge from his time in the military, used to gain trust

## Key Abilities

- **Fighting Spirit** — Grants temporary hit points and advantage on attacks
- **Sharpshooter** — Deadly precision with ranged attacks at the cost of accuracy
- **Second Wind** — Quick self-healing in combat

## Adventures

### The Escape (Session 1)
Voris was among the first arrested at [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep). He fought alongside [Flint](/characters/flint), [Peren](/characters/peren), and [Thia](/characters/thia) to [escape](/events/escape-from-morgans-keep) the capital and flee toward [Rinden](/places/rinden).

### Dior & the Crypt (Sessions 6–8)
In [Dior](/places/dior), Voris negotiated the sale of magical glasses at [the Academy](/places/the-academy), explored shops with [Bob Swords](/characters/bob-swords), and had his bow repaired by [Tore Idle](/characters/tore-idle). He then ventured into [the Crypt](/places/the-crypt) with [Carlos](/characters/carlos) as guide, where the party fought a fire elemental and encountered a dragon. Voris used his Song of the Forest flute to summon spectral water spirits that drove the elemental away.

### Helga Confrontation (Sessions 11–12)
Voris shot out [Helga](/characters/helga)'s lantern with a dramatic arrow at the [Tyr Moir](/places/tyr-moir) graveyard, then rappelled from a rooftop for a theatrical entrance. He took the lead in interrogating Helga about the [Crown Prince murder](/events/crown-prince-murder).

### Journey to Morgan's Keep (Session 14)
Traveling with [Peren](/characters/peren), Voris survived a poisoning and ambush at an inn. He met [Lynn Salt](/characters/lynn-salt) and an old man named Thor, sharing stories over cheese before continuing toward [Morgan's Keep](/places/morgans-keep).

## Relationships

- **[Peren](/characters/peren)** — Close traveling companion; they often operate as a pair
- **[Fenris](/characters/fenris)** — Fellow crypt explorer; Fenris often provides magical support for Voris's attacks
- **[Helga](/characters/helga)** — Antagonistic; she arrested him and he confronted her at the graveyard
